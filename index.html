FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my website?
+
Starter sites are typically ready in 3 days. Growth packages take 5–7 days. Authority packages take 10–14 days. We share a clear timeline before we start.
Do I need to pay hosting fees?
+
No! We host your website on GitHub Pages which is completely free and highly reliable. You only pay for a custom domain (e.g. yourname.in) which costs about ₹700–₹1000/year.
What if I want changes after delivery?
+
All packages include a revision round. Growth includes 1 month of free support and Authority includes 3 months. Beyond that, we offer affordable maintenance retainers.
Do you write the website content too?
+
Growth and Authority packages include conversion-focused copywriting. For Starter, we work from content you provide — but we'll guide you on what to write.
Can I add more pages later?
+
Absolutely. The codebase we deliver is clean and modular — you or we can add pages anytime. We also provide a guide on how to manage the site yourself.
